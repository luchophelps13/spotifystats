    • Hello There! This is a project developed by Lucas Phillips. It uses the Spotify API, Spotipy, and various graphing libraries to give the user a strong understanding of their listening history, including top artists, recent artists, and song data. Furthermore, new artists are recommended based of the user's top artists. If you test every feature, you will leave with a greater understanding of your music taste and have a playlist generated for you :)
